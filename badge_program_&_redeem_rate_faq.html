Badge Program & Redeem Rate - FAQ

The conversion rate is the amount of money you can withdraw for each Yellow Rose you earn. You can increase your rate by participating in our Badge Program — higher badges mean better conversion rates, so the more active and engaged you are, the more you can earn.
To receive your referral reward, the friend you referred must earn at least 20 Yellow Roses and complete their KYC. We strictly discourage fraudulent behavior. If our fraud team detects any suspicious activity, your referral reward may be placed on hold.
