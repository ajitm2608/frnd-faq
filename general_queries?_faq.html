From the home screen, simply tap the Go Online button to start hosting. You can then begin one-on-one audio or video calls and connect with users in real time.
Here are some tips to boost your calls on FRND: Stay online longer – Be online for at least 1–2 hours. If you’re offline, you won’t get notifications about your room. Prefer audio calls – Audio calls tend to attract more users than video calls. Be active at peak times – Most users join after 6 PM, with the busiest hours between 7 PM and 2 AM. Use these timings to your advantage. Leverage the network effect – The more people you talk to, the more they’ll share their experience with others, bringing you more calls. Consistency is key! Stay active, stay consistent, and watch your calls grow.
If you see the Invalid Game ID error, try this: Go to your Profile. Open Settings. Select Logout from all devices. Log in again using your same phone number. This should fix the issue and let you join rooms smoothly.
You can find users you’ve connected with earlier in the Recent tab. Just go there, and you’ll see a list of FRNDs you’ve previously interacted with — making it easy to reconnect.
